Hello, I’m Aby Kuruvilla — a UI designer and front-end developer focused on creating intuitive and visually refined digital products. I design and build modern web and mobile interfaces that balance usability, performance, and aesthetics. My process covers the full product journey: research, wireframing, UI design, prototyping, and responsive front-end development. With experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I bridge the gap between design and development to ensure ideas translate into polished, functional interfaces.

Beyond design,My approach focuses on clarity, usability, and detail, ensuring that every project not only looks great but also works seamlessly for users. I enjoy collaborating with clients to create digital products that are both visually engaging and practical.
